Welcome to Peace & Joy

7.3

Nestled down a quiet street on Lonla Tole Marga in Patan, Lalitpur, Peace and Joy Guest House is a recently renovated, adults-only property designed for travelers seeking cozy accommodations, absolute comfort, and authentic local vibes.

Located just a 6-minute walk from the spectacular Patan Durbar Square UNESCO World Heritage site, our guest house features parquet-floored rooms with private bathrooms, silent air conditioning, free high-speed Wi-Fi, and work desks. Guests can relax on our sun terrace, cook in the shared kitchen, and take advantage of daily housekeeping, bicycle parking, car hire, and full-day security.

Patan Durbar Square
5 min walk

A beautiful UNESCO world heritage square packed with royal palaces, wood-carved windows, and the ancient stone Krishna Mandir.

The Golden Temple
7 min walk

An intricate 12th-century Buddhist monastery, famous for its golden facade, prayer wheels, and serene spiritual environment.

Mahaboudha Temple
10 min walk

Often referred to as the temple of 1000 Buddhas. Every single terracotta brick has a unique carving of Buddha.

Pim Bahal Pond
8 min walk

A quiet, local reservoir featuring a gorgeous stupa. Locals gather around the water in the evenings to enjoy tea and talk.
